About Me

With a deep passion for high-quality software testing and a strong foundation in full-stack development, I continuously develop myself as a Full Stack Automation Engineer in Test. My broad experience across various domains, combined with my strong problem-solving abilities and meticulous attention to detail, enables me to deliver effective and reliable automation solutions.

Skills and Experience:

  • Test Automation: I have developed and maintained comprehensive test frameworks using Selenium, Appium, and TestNG. I automate test scenarios for both web and mobile applications. Additionally, I utilize Cucumber to implement BDD (Behavior Driven Development) principles, ensuring that test scenarios align with business requirements.
  • Database Technologies: Proficient in JDBC and SQL, I have experience managing and querying relational databases such as PostgreSQL. This expertise allows me to effectively validate data integrity and performance during the testing process.
  • Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and Software Testing Life Cycle (STLC): I have a deep understanding of SDLC and STLC principles, which enables me to enhance test strategies and execution processes. I apply these principles to improve the overall effectiveness of the testing lifecycle.
  • Web Technologies: Experienced in frontend technologies such as HTML and CSS, I am skilled in creating and executing test cases that ensure the seamless functionality and user experience of web applications.
  • Manual Testing: In addition to automation, I have a solid background in manual testing, which complements automated tests with detailed exploratory and functional testing approaches.
  • Tools and Collaboration: Proficient in using Jira for issue tracking and project management, and GitHub for version control and collaboration. These tools support my effective participation in team environments and contribute to efficient workflow management.

My career goal is to continuously develop and implement innovative test automation solutions while staying current with emerging technologies and methodologies. I am committed to achieving the highest standards of quality in every project and making a significant impact on the software development lifecycle.
